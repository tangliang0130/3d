The Maison

We don't hang work
on walls. We build rooms
around it.

Restraint is the discipline of luxury. Every surface, every shadow at ICG is composed so the object can breathe — warm lime plaster, charcoal stone, and a single shaft of northern light. Nothing competes. Everything resonates.
